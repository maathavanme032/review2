2023 KTM 390 ADVENTURES SPOKE WHEEL INDIA LAUNCH


WHAT HARDCORE DOES THE KTM 390 WIRE SPOKE USED IN IT


This motorcycle comes equipped with spoked wheels and black anodized aluminium rims instead of alloy units on the lower versions. These retain the 19-inch front and 17-inch rear combination. However, unlike the alloy wheels, the wire-spoke units are wrapped in tube tyres. This variant also benefits from an updated suspension setup that comprises adjustable front forks and a rear monoshock. The front forks feature compression (30 clicks) and rebound (30 clicks) adjustability, while the rear monoshock is rebound (20 clicks) and preload (10-step) adjustable. Meanwhile, the travel rating for the front forks and rear monoshock is rated at 170mm and 177mm, respectively. On the other hand, the braking setup remains unaltered, and this variant of the KTM 390 Adventure retains a 320mm front disc and 230mm single discs with Bybre-sourced callipers.
